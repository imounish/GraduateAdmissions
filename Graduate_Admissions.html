Graduate Admissions¶

This Jupyter notebook is aimed at predicting the probability of admission into Universities for Graduation.
The dataset can be obtained from https://www.kaggle.com/mohansacharya/graduate-admissions
The notebook is divided into 4 sections, namely Importing and Reading the Dataset, Visualizing the Correlation among the features, Regression Algorithms, Classification Algorithms.
Only the algorithms with higher accuracy are included. Will be adding other Algorithms later. This notebook is inspired and written by following this python notebook: https://www.kaggle.com/hidede/graduate-admissions

Features in the dataset:

  • GRE Scores (290 to 340)
  • TOEFL Scores (92 to 120)
  • University Rating (1 to 5)
  • Statement of Purpose (1 to 5)
  • Letter of Recommendation Strength (1 to 5)
  • Undergraduate CGPA (6.8 to 9.92)
  • Research Experience (0 or 1)
  • Chance of Admit (0.34 to 0.97)
